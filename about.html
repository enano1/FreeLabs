Our Story

In response to the urgent challenges facing the education sector, we present Free Labs, a groundbreaking project designed specifically for the Spark! Mini-Hack event. Free Labs aims to provide free, interactive virtual labs that enhance motivation, build confidence, and personalize learning, promoting equitable access to education and empowering educators through technology integration.

Free Labs is a collaborative effort initiated by Shashank Ramachandran, Sungjun Park, Paul Enano, Max Greenspan, and Grace Wang.

We are committed to enhancing the accessibility of education for all. You can connect with us through:
